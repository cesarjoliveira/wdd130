Cesar Jose de Oliveira | WDD 130

I'm a Brazilian happy guy. I love music and having time with my family. I Borned at Brazilian Northeast in a small city named "Bezerros" but as soon as I turn 6 months old my family moved to Sao Paulo and I lived there until I turn 18. Then I served a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints at Manaus Brazil. I feel blessed for serving a mission. Curently I work as a data engineer. As a data engineer I work a lot of business and personal data, designing and implementing efficient data pipelines. Despite my professional focus, my heart remains tied to Brazil, where I was born and spent my formative years. Serving a mission in Manaus was a transformative experience, and now as a data engineer, I find joy in balancing work and family. In the midst of crafting data symphonies as a data engineer, I still sway to the vibrant tunes of my Brazilian origins. Serving a mission in Manaus not only broadened my horizons but also added unique notes to the rhythm of my life, where work and family compose a harmonious melody.
